About RepoDetox

RepoDetox is a modern and responsive web app for managing your GitHub following list. It helps you discover which users you follow aren't following you back, and lets you unfollow them with a single click.

  • 🔍 Search & Filter: Quickly find users in your list.
  • 🌈 Sleek Ranking: See who you followed recently, with colorful ranking badges.
  • 🖱️ Mass Unfollow: Select and unfollow several users at once.
  • 🖱️ One-Click Unfollow: Clean up your following list effortlessly.
  • 🔒 Privacy-First: Your credentials never leave your browser.
  • 👀 Followers Page: View all users who follow you!
  • 🌗 Theme Toggle: Switch between Light and Dark modes anytime.

Open Source: Check out the project on GitHub.
